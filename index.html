Why Learn?

The onset and recent prevalence of virtual reality adds a new dimension to our digital experience, and brings numerous opportunities to visually enhance the data exploration experience. Data exploration capabilities of any virtual reality framework, however, rely on the backend data system, which stores, operates on, and curates data. We co-design data systems with virtual reality devices aimed at exploring huge data sets.
