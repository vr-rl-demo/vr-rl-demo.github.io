Collect and Curate Information

ExploVR continuously speculates and collects strategic cues, such as descriptive statistics and trend estimators and provides them alongside data at the required frame rate to the VR front end.

Learn from User Interactions

As data scientists use ExploVR to explore data, every interaction - navigation, button presses, and gestures - is considered as an opportunity to directly refine and tune various internal parameters such as the granularity of collected information and part of a data set to target.
